About Us

We are a team of students studying Bioinformatics at the Technical University / Ludwig-Maximilians Universitiy

in Munich.

We are driven to learn and explore new possibilities in computer science and biology.

Right now we work as a team to create an easy to use scientific paper scraper that gains its information from multiple databases.

This is the final project of our Problem Based Learning seminar.
